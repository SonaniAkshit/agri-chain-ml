### 1. Geo-Political Page: Key Components (The "Logic")

Kisan ko complex news nahi, balki uska **Direct Impact** chahiye. Hum ye 3 cheezein dikhayenge:
1.  **Event Cards:** News ki short summary (e.g., Russia-Ukraine War).
2.  **Trade Status:** Export/Import par kya asar hua? (Red/Green indicators).
3.  **Impact Marker on Graph:** Price graph par ek "Marker" (jhanda) hoga jo dikhayega ki is event ke baad price kitna jump hua.

### 3. Logic Building (Zero to Google Level)

* **News to Data:** Hum **GNews API** ya **RSS Feeds** use karenge "Agriculture" keywords ke saath.
* **The "Reasoning" (Taran):** Hum ek simple logic table banayenge:
    * *If Export Stops -> Local Supply High -> Price Down (initially).*
    * *If Global Shortage -> Export Demand High -> Price Up.*
* **NLP (Natural Language Processing):** Google perspective se, hum **Sentiment Analysis** use kar sakte hain news headlines par, taaki system apne aap detect kare ki news "Positive" hai ya "Negative".

### 4. Google Interview Perspective: "External Factors"
Interviewer puchega: *"How do you handle data that is not in your database, like sudden news?"*

**Tera Answer:**
> "Bhai, maine ek **Event-Driven Architecture** design ki hai. Hum news APIs se keywords match karte hain. Agar 'Export Ban' jaise keyword milte hain, toh hamara model use **Weighted Feature** ki tarah input leta hai aur price forecast ko 'Adjust' kar deta hai. Ise **External Shocks Modeling** kehte hain."
